Engage

In addition to presenting, you also have a role as an active participant in the symposium. Your engagement with your peers is a valuable opportunity to expand your understanding, exchange ideas, and contribute to the collective learning experience.

  1. Watch: Pay close attention to your peers ' presentations, noting key insights, strengths, and areas for further discussion.
  2. Reflect: Note any questions or comments that highlight connections to course concepts or explore deeper perspectives on their work.
  3. Discuss: Participate in the post-presentation Project Check-In discussion to share your observations, ask questions, and provide constructive feedback.
