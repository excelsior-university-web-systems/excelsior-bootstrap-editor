In this discussion, you will reflect on your immersive project's progress, share any challenges or successes you encountered, and learn from your classmate's experiences.

When you're ready to begin, navigate to the Post tab for detailed instructions on how to make your initial post. Check the Reply tab to review the requirements for responding to your peers. Lastly, visit the Evaluation tab for details on this discussion's expectations and grading.

Post

For your initial post in this discussion, address the discussion prompt and follow the discussion guidelines accompanying it. Your initial post is due by Wednesday, at 11:59 PM ET.

Discussion Prompt

For your initial post in this discussion, provide a project update that includes the following details:

  • Project title and team members (if applicable)
  • Summary of Progress: Briefly describe what progress has been made since the last check-in, and highlight any specific milestones.
  • Challenges and Solutions: Discuss any obstacles encountered and explain how you've addressed them or your plans to overcome them.
  • Questions or Feedback Requests: Share any specific questions or requests for feedback on any aspect of your project.

Review the Evaluation tab for details on this discussion's expectations and grading.

  Note: If you are working with a team for your project, please collaborate to craft your initial project update. Then, choose one individual to post it to the forum. All team members should actively respond to updates and responses from other teams in the forum.

  Tip: Consider using the Online Tutoring and Anti-Plagiarism resources on the left panel to write, check your work, and correct any spelling or grammatical errors. You can also visit the Discussion Posting Guide for more information and support.

Reply

Read your classmates' original posts and reply to at least two. As you craft your responses, consider doing the following:

  • Offer constructive feedback or suggestions
  • Share relevant experiences or resources that may be helpful to your peers
  • Ask thoughtful questions to help your peers reflect on their projects

Provide two thoughtful and courteous responses by Sunday at 11:59 PM ET. 

You can choose to record a video response to complete this assignment. Written posts are equally acceptable. If you choose to record a video, your video should be two to five minutes long.

  Reminder: Be thoughtful and courteous in your interactions with others in discussions. Consider the Excelsior University Student Conduct policy as it relates to these interactions.

Evaluation

  Expectations

Your initial post is due Wednesday, at 11:59 PM ET; all discussion activities including your replies to classmates' posts must be completed by Saturday, at 11:59 PM ET. Failure to meet the timeliness and quantity expectations will result in a point deduction.

  Grading

This is a graded discussion. To ensure that your discussion posts and replies meet the expectations and participation criteria, please, consult the Discussion Posting Guide and the Discussion Rubric.

  Tip: To view the discussion rubric, select the Options (three dots) icon in the upper right and select Show Rubric. Review the Canvas Guide on finding discussion rubrics for additional details.
